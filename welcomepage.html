About Me

What made me choose computer science was actually a movie called "The Internship" which was about an internship at Google and it lead me to computer science. I looked into it a bunch and thought it could totally be something I could try and took my first programming class junior year of high school and loved it. Now here I am 4 years later and still rocking with computer science.
